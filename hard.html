1. Choose a difficulty on the landing page: Easy, Medium, or Hard.

2. A hidden dessert word will appear as blank spaces (_ _ _).

3. Type one letter in the input box and press the Guess button.

4. If the letter is in the word, it will appear in the correct spot.

5. If the letter is NOT in the word, you lose one guess and the cake gets eaten!

6. The letters you already guessed will appear in the "Used Letters" section.

7. Guess the full dessert word before you run out of guesses to win!
